</div><p class="copy--feature">Want to try something new for your next trip? Push your family out of their collective comfort zone with these ideas for an adventure you can have together.</p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Iceland-9d16261414f9.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/family-adventure-destinations&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Iceland-9d16261414f9.jpg&amp;description=iceland" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-106421 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Iceland-9d16261414f9.jpg" alt="iceland"> A family hike along a glacier in Iceland © Henn Photography / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">1. Iceland</h3><p>Travel to <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/iceland" class="copy--body__link">Iceland</a> and within one trip your family can go dog-sledding, whale watching and glacier trekking, as well as see the Northern Lights and spend plenty of time jumping in and out of thermal pools. If that isn’t adventurous enough for you, how about descending into the bowels of an active volcano?</p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/New-Zealand-6af03cbd9dbb.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/family-adventure-destinations&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/New-Zealand-6af03cbd9dbb.jpg&amp;description=new-zealand" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-106424 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/New-Zealand-6af03cbd9dbb.jpg" alt="new-zealand"> For the ultimate outdoor adventure, explore New Zealand's natural beauty by camper van © David Wong / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">2. New Zealand</h3><p><a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/new-zealand" class="copy--body__link">New Zealand</a>’s natural beauty and excellent reputation for outdoor pursuits make it a popular destination for families. Hiring a camper van is not only an economical way to explore the country but also gives you the freedom of the open road and the fun of outdoor life without having to put a tent up each night.</p><div class="halfway-links--related media">

Marrakesh, Morocco</h3><p>The call to prayer and snake charmers plying their trade; tiny alleys to explore; shops with wares piled high; spices, tagines and fresh juices to sample – a visit to the medina in <a href="http://marrakesh" class="copy--body__link">Marrakesh</a> is an adventure for the senses. It’s also a great opportunity to learn an important skill of travelling: the art of haggling.</p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/South-Africa-2d7ed9b0b53f.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/family-adventure-destinations&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/South-Africa-2d7ed9b0b53f.jpg&amp;description=south-africa" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-106319 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/South-Africa-2d7ed9b0b53f.jpg" alt="south-africa"> Kruger National Park is the perfect place for kids to have an unforgettable animal encounter © moodboard / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">4. South Africa</h3><p>Seeing animals in the wild scores pretty high on the adventure meter for most people. <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/south-africa" class="copy--body__link">South Africa</a>’s Kruger National Park is recommended for children due to the high likelihood of spotting animals and the relatively small distances involved in travelling round the southern part of the park. There are also family-friendly lodges with pools for when everyone needs to cool off.</p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Snowdonia-1a74d9e57bb9.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/family-adventure-destinations&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Snowdonia-1a74d9e57bb9.jpg&amp;description=snowdonia" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-107804 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Snowdonia-1a74d9e57bb9.jpg" alt="snowdonia"> Bounce beneath the Earth's surface on subterranean trampolines © James Smart / Captured on a Samsung Galaxy</p><h3 class="copy--h3">5. Snowdonia, UK</h3><p>‘Bounce below’ on trampolines hidden within caves, fly through the air on a giant swing and surf an inland lagoon. It’s not hard to see why North Wales made our Top Destinations list for 2017. Kids will also love adventuring through history at the many castles, climbing to the top of <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/wales/snowdonia-national-park-parc-cenedlaethol-eryri" class="copy--body__link">Snowdon</a> and resting weary legs on a narrow-gauge railway or two.</p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Washington-3b3672c67296.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/family-adventure-destinations&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Washington-3b3672c67296.jpg&amp;description=The%20Jefferson%20Memorial%20and%20Tidal%20Basin%20with%20cherry%20blossoms%20in%20full%20bloom." class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-106320 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Washington-3b3672c67296.jpg" alt="The Jefferson Memorial and Tidal Basin with cherry blossoms in full bloom."> Cherry blossom blooms around the Tidal Basin and Jefferson Memorial in Washington DC © Dwight Nadig / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">6. Washington, DC, USA</h3><p>Exploring a world-famous city is an adventure in itself. In <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/usa/washington-dc" class="copy--body__link">DC</a> you can combine learning the art of espionage (at the International Spy Museum) with a dose of history (the Lincoln Memorial, plus a range of excellent museums) and lots of fun (the elevator ride to the top of the Washington Monument, paddle boating in the Tidal Basin).</p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Australia-242d7c68b1e6.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/family-adventure-destinations&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Australia-242d7c68b1e6.jpg&amp;description=australia" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-106316 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Australia-242d7c68b1e6.jpg" alt="australia"> Indulge your inner Tarzan with a treetop walk in the Valley of the Giants © EAGiven / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">7. Southwestern Australia</h3><p>Do your kids have their heads in the clouds? Treat them to a 600m-long treetop walk in the Valley of the Giants. Or do they prefer to go underground? Check out the fascinating caves in the <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/australia/western-australia/margaret-river" class="copy--body__link">Margaret River</a> region. Is wildlife their thing? Go whale watching in Geographe Bay. Beach combing? Bike riding? Tree climbing? You’ve guessed it…</p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Japan-23f1118237f8.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/family-adventure-destinations&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Japan-23f1118237f8.jpg&amp;description=japan" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-106422 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Japan-23f1118237f8.jpg" alt="japan"> The bright lights of Tokyo are a big draw for kids and adults alike © kokoroimages.com / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">8. Japan</h3><p>For robot-loving, game-playing, tech-happy teens a trip to <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/japan" class="copy--body__link">Japan</a> is a dream come true. Add some cerebral pursuits – discover manga and anime together or visit ancient temples and shrines – and include a visit to one of the national parks or island beaches for when everyone needs to breathe out. Before you know it, you’ve got a trip to please even the most reluctant adventurers in your family.</p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Sri-Lanka-29a232504507.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/family-adventure-destinations&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Sri-Lanka-29a232504507.jpg&amp;description=sri-lanka" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-106425 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Sri-Lanka-29a232504507.jpg" alt="sri-lanka"> A family of elephants chow down in the Minneriya Wildlife Reserve, Sri Lanka © fmajor / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">9. Sri Lanka</h3><p>Between elephants and trains, ancient temples and beaches, forts and natural parks, there’s plenty to keep families busy in this little part of the Indian subcontinent. Even better, Sri Lankans welcome children with open arms, so – with a little planning and an appetite for adventure – travelling here is a rewarding experience for all.</p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Malaysia-88535ea4d378.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/family-adventure-destinations&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Malaysia-88535ea4d378.jpg&amp;description=malaysia" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-106318 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/Malaysia-88535ea4d378.jpg" alt="malaysia"> A father and son go snorkelling in the crystal-clear water around Malaysia © Maridav / Shutterstock</p><h3 class="copy--h3">10. Malaysia</h3><p>Whether you go for beaches, jungle or one of the child-friendly cities, exploring <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/malaysia" class="copy--body__link">Malaysia</a> is a great way to introduce Southeast Asia to your kids. The mix of cultures allows just enough of the familiar (colonial architecture, shopping centres, Western food) for when the exotic (the heat, the flavours, the bustle) becomes overwhelming.</p></div>
