</div><p class="copy--feature">African safaris can cost upwards of US$1000 per person per day – but don't scratch them off your travel wish list yet. There are ways to see the big five and more on a budget, if you know where to look.</p><p>This extract from our new title, <em><a href="http://shop.lonelyplanet.com/world/the-best-things-in-life-are-free/" class="copy--body__link">The Best Things in Life are Free</a>, </em>reveals destinations where the wild rewards vastly outdo the price tag.</p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Lion-and-lioness-in-Kruger-National-Park-%C2%AB-Thomas-Retterath-Getty-Images-Flickr-7f1405633440.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/safari-without-the-big-bucks&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Lion-and-lioness-in-Kruger-National-Park-%25C2%25AB-Thomas-Retterath-Getty-Images-Flickr-7f1405633440.jpg&amp;description=Lion%20and%20lioness%20in%20Kruger%20National%20Park%20%C2%A9%20Thomas%20Retterath%20/%20Getty%20Images" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-104080 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Lion-and-lioness-in-Kruger-National-Park-%C2%AB-Thomas-Retterath-Getty-Images-Flickr-7f1405633440.jpg" alt="Lion and lioness in Kruger National Park © Thomas Retterath / Getty Images"> A lion and lioness in Kruger National Park © Thomas Retterath / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">Big five safari – South Africa</h3><p>Kruger is one of the world’s greatest parks – the Big Five are all here, along with an extensive cast of other safari species. Share the park’s vehicle hire (US$20 per hour) with others to make it more of a bargain. Budget accommodation too.</p><p><em><a href="http://www.molemotelgh.com/" target="_blank" class="copy--body__link">Molemotelgh.com</a>; Mole National Park, <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/ghana" class="copy--body__link">Ghana</a>; US$8, plus guide US$2.50/hr.</em></p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Etosha-%C2%AE-Hannes-Thirion-Getty-Images-1-7aa78d81537b.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/safari-without-the-big-bucks&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Etosha-%25C2%25AE-Hannes-Thirion-Getty-Images-1-7aa78d81537b.jpg&amp;description=Spot%20a%20leopard%20in%20Etosha%20National%20Park%20%C2%A9%20Hannes%20Thirion%20/%20Getty%20Images" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-104084 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Etosha-%C2%AE-Hannes-Thirion-Getty-Images-1-7aa78d81537b.jpg" alt="Spot a leopard in Etosha National Park © Hannes Thirion / Getty Images"> Spot a leopard in Etosha National Park © Hannes Thirion / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">Namibian safari – Namibia</h3><p>The desert-like environment around the massive Etosha salt pan is captivating, but it is the wildlife that frequents the waterholes here that make it <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/namibia/northwestern-namibia/etosha-national-park" class="copy--body__link">one of Africa’s best parks</a>. Great roads mean small rental cars are an option, and camping is possible.</p><p><em><a href="http://www.etoshanationalpark.org/" target="_blank" class="copy--body__link">Etoshanationalpark.org</a>; Etosha National Park, Namibia; US$7.</em></p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Cycle-sarafi-Kenya-%C2%AE-mgokalp-Getty-Images-1-c4e4f933aa74.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/safari-without-the-big-bucks&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Cycle-sarafi-Kenya-%25C2%25AE-mgokalp-Getty-Images-1-c4e4f933aa74.jpg&amp;description=Earn%20your%20safari%20stripes%20on%20a%20cycle%20safari%20in%20Kenya%20%C2%A9%20mgokalp%20/%20Getty%20Images" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-104082 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Cycle-sarafi-Kenya-%C2%AE-mgokalp-Getty-Images-1-c4e4f933aa74.jpg" alt="Earn your safari stripes on a cycle safari in Kenya © mgokalp / Getty Images"> Earn your safari stripes on a cycle safari in Kenya © mgokalp / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">Cycle safari – Kenya</h3><p>Beneath the basalt cliffs of this volcanic landscape are zebras, giraffes, impalas and other iconic species. Large carnivores are rare, however, so it’s possible to spend a day walking or cycling; bikes can be rented nearby for an additional US$6. Camping is available.</p><p><em><a href="http://www.kws.go.ke/" target="_blank" class="copy--body__link">Kws.go.ke</a>; Hell’s Gate National Park, <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/kenya" class="copy--body__link">Kenya</a>; US$30.</em></p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Elephants-Okavango-Delta-Botswana-%C2%AB-Mint-Images-Getty-Images-1-88910311d399.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/safari-without-the-big-bucks&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Elephants-Okavango-Delta-Botswana-%25C2%25AB-Mint-Images-Getty-Images-1-88910311d399.jpg&amp;description=Elephants%20drink%20from%20the%20Okavango%20Delta,%20Botswana%20%C2%A9%20Mint%20Images%20/%20Getty%20Images" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-104085 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Elephants-Okavango-Delta-Botswana-%C2%AB-Mint-Images-Getty-Images-1-88910311d399.jpg" alt="Elephants drink from the Okavango Delta, Botswana © Mint Images / Getty Images"> Elephants drink from the Okavango Delta, Botswana © Mint Images / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">Okavango Delta mokoro safari – Botswana</h3><p>Being poled through the <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/botswana/okavango-delta" class="copy--body__link">Okavango Delta</a> in a dugout canoe is one of Africa’s greatest, and most expensive, experiences. However, Old Bridge Backpackers offer self-catered camping options ranging from US$70 (one day) to US$140 (four days).</p><p><em><a href="https://maun-backpackers.com/" target="_blank" class="copy--body__link">Maun-backpackers.com</a>; The latter is a classic Big Five destination, while the former hosts both land animals and aquatic ones. Budget safaris and accommodation available.</p><p><em>St Lucia, <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/south-africa/kwazulu-natal" target="_blank" class="copy--body__link">KwaZulu-Natal</a>, South Africa; from US$40.</em></p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Bwindi-%C2%AE-mick789-Getty-Images-1-30e4e7e1c306.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/safari-without-the-big-bucks&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Bwindi-%25C2%25AE-mick789-Getty-Images-1-30e4e7e1c306.jpg&amp;description=Come%20face%20to%20face%20with%20mountain%20gorillas%20in%20Rwanda%C2%A0%C2%A9%20mick789%20/%20Getty%20Images" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-104087 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Bwindi-%C2%AE-mick789-Getty-Images-1-30e4e7e1c306.jpg" alt="Come face to face with mountain gorillas in Rwanda&nbsp;© mick789 / Getty Images"> Come face to face with mountain gorillas in Rwanda&nbsp;© mick789 / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">Tracking gorillas – Uganda</h3><p>The value of sharing a glance with a gorilla in the wild is something that can’t be quantified. Visit <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/uganda/western-uganda/bwindi-impenetrable-national-park" class="copy--body__link">Bwindi</a> during low season (April, May and November) and permits are US$350 instead of US$600. Permits in Rwanda are US$750.</p><p><em><a href="http://www.ugandawildlife.org/" target="_blank" class="copy--body__link">Ugandawildlife.org</a>; Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/uganda" class="copy--body__link">Uganda</a>; from US$350.</em></p></div>
