A demanding exercise for experienced walkers, it’s also cross-country skiable in winter.</p><p><em><a href="http://parkweb.vic.gov.au/" class="copy--body__link">Parkweb.vic.gov.au</a>; Victoria, <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/australia" class="copy--body__link">Australia</a>; 36km; free.</em></p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Cape-to-Cape-%C2%AE-J_Knaupe-Getty-Images-1-77786401d3d3.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/australia-and-new-zealands-best-free-walks&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Cape-to-Cape-%25C2%25AE-J_Knaupe-Getty-Images-1-77786401d3d3.jpg&amp;description=Pray%20for%20a%20blister-free%20experience%20on%20this%20epic%20week-long%20walk%20%C2%A9%20J_Knaupe%20/%20Getty%20Images" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-104011 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Cape-to-Cape-%C2%AE-J_Knaupe-Getty-Images-1-77786401d3d3.jpg" alt="Pray for a blister-free experience on this epic week-long walk © J_Knaupe / Getty Images"> Pray for a blister-free experience on this epic week-long walk © J_Knaupe / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">Cape-to-Cape</h3><p>Running between Cape Naturaliste and Cape Leeuwin, this lighthouse-to-lighthouse seven-day epic is one colossal coastal walk, promising everything from whale sightings to wine tasting. Wild camping is possible.</p><p><em><a href="http://www.capetocapetrack.com.au/" class="copy--body__link">Capetocapetrack.com.au</a>; <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/australia/western-australia" class="copy--body__link">Western Australia</a>; 135km; free.</em></p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Bartle-%C2%A9-Neal-Pritchard-Photography-Getty-Images-1-da34e328602f.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/australia-and-new-zealands-best-free-walks&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Bartle-%25C2%25A9-Neal-Pritchard-Photography-Getty-Images-1-da34e328602f.jpg&amp;description=Josephine%20Falls,%20Queensland,%20Australia%20%C2%A9%20Neal%20Pritchard%20Photography%20/%20Getty%20Images" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-104013 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Bartle-%C2%A9-Neal-Pritchard-Photography-Getty-Images-1-da34e328602f.jpg" alt="Josephine Falls, Queensland, Australia © Neal Pritchard Photography / Getty Images"> Josephine Falls, Queensland, Australia © Neal Pritchard Photography / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">Bartle Frere Trail</h3><p>From Josephine Falls, 75km south of Cairns, return-hike to <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/australia/queensland" class="copy--body__link">Queensland</a>’s highest point. Tropical trails climb dramatically through lush rainforest until you pop out of the canopy and cop a view across the Tablelands to the Great Barrier Reef, vaguely visible through the iridescence of the Coral Sea.</p><p><em><a href="http://www.nprsr.qld.gov.au/parks/bartle-frere/about.html" class="copy--body__link">Nprsr.qld.gov.au/parks/bartle-frere</a>;
