Viggiano / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">Lake Walchen – Germany</h3><p><a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/germany/bavaria" class="copy--body__link">Bavaria</a> is blessed with a batch of brilliant bathing spots, and this deep alpine lake in the mountains south of Munich is one of the most spectacular. Wilder spots can be found too – some with great cliff jumps.</p><p><em><a href="http://en.lac-annecy.com/" target="_blank" class="copy--body__link">Lac-annecy.com</a>; Haute-Savoie, France; free.</em></p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Fiastra-%C2%AE-Buffy1982-Getty-Images-fe4cbd281867.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/europes-best-wild-swimming&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Fiastra-%25C2%25AE-Buffy1982-Getty-Images-fe4cbd281867.jpg&amp;description=Take%20a%20dip%20in%20Lago%20di%20Fiastra,%20Italy%20%C2%A9%20Buffy1982%20/%20Getty%20Images" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-104073 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/06/Fiastra-%C2%AE-Buffy1982-Getty-Images-fe4cbd281867.jpg" alt="Take a dip in Lago di Fiastra, Italy © Buffy1982 / Getty Images"> Take a dip in Lago di Fiastra, Italy © Buffy1982 / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">Lago di Fiastra – Italy</h3><p>Camp beneath mountains on the north shore of this stunning beach-lined lake, and slide into the embrace of its gin-clear water.
