For a glacier you can walk up to, visit Matanuska, a 27-mile/43-km tongue of ice poking out from the Chugach Mountains.</p><p><em>Free.</em></p><h3 class="copy--h3">Nordic skiing</h3><p>Forget expensive lift passes, extortionate on-mountain accommodation rates and crowded runs, try your arm (and legs) at Nordic skiing somewhere like Higgins Lake, <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/usa/great-lakes/michigan" class="copy--body__link">Michigan</a>, where you can ski a groomed 12-mile/19-km network of trails for just $8 per day.</p><p class="stack__article__image-container"><a href="/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/GettyImages-103059925_super-1661072857dc.jpg" class="copy--body__link"></a><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-tips-and-articles/top-free-wheelin-all-american-adventures&amp;media=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/GettyImages-103059925_super-1661072857dc.jpg&amp;description=What%20will%20you%20find%20beneath%20the%20surface%20in%20Florida%20Keys%20National%20Marine%20Sanctuary?%20%C2%A9%20Image%20Source%20/%20Getty%20Images" class="social__item social__item--pinterest social__item--pinned" target="blank">  <span class="accessibility">Pin this image</span>  <span class="social__icon icon--pinterest icon--white"></span></a><img class="wp-image-104414 size-full stack__article__image is-landscape" src="//images-resrc.staticlp.com/S=W748/O=75/http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travel-blog/tip-article/wordpress_uploads/2016/07/GettyImages-103059925_super-1661072857dc.jpg" alt="What will you find beneath the surface in Florida Keys National Marine Sanctuary? © Image Source / Getty Images"> What will you find beneath the surface in Florida Keys National Marine Sanctuary? © Image Source / Getty Images</p><h3 class="copy--h3">Life aquatic</h3><p>Armed with fins and a snorkel set, explore Florida Keys National Marine Sanctuary, which protects the planet’s third-largest reef and contains the submerged statue Christ of the Abyss.</p><p><em><a href="http://floridakeys.noaa.gov/" target="_blank" class="copy--body__link">Floridakeys.noaa.gov</a>; Key Largo, Florida; free.</em></p><h3 class="copy--h3">Body-surfing</h3><p>No board? No money? No worries! <a href="http://www.lonelyplanet.com/usa/honolulu-and-waikiki/sights/beaches-islands-waterfronts/kaka-ako-waterfront-park-3-1451570" class="copy--body__link">Point Panic beach</a> in Hawai’i, in the midst of a surfing mecca, has a wave so tailor-made for body-surfing that boarders stay away, leaving it to the penny-pinching purists.</p><p><em><a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/usa/hawaii" class="copy--body__link">Hawai’i</a>, HI; free.</em></p><h3 class="copy--h3">Canyoneering</h3><p>A non-technical introduction to an addictive art, the spectacular Peekaboo–Spooky Gulch Loop and Escalante-Grand Staircase National Monument in <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/usa/southwest/utah" class="copy--body__link">Utah</a> can be spliced into easy 3-mile/5-km (three-hour) return scrambles, with plenty of slots and arches to explore.</p><p><em><a href="http://utah.com/hiking" target="_blank" class="copy--body__link">Utah.com/hiking</a>; Lake Powell’s extensive shoreline is the perfect venue.</p><p><em><a href="http://www.lakepowell.com/" target="_blank" class="copy--body__link">Lakepowell.com</a>; Glen Canyon National Recreation Area, UT; free.</em></p><h3 class="copy--h3">Trail-running</h3><p>In autumn, go trail-running around <a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/usa/new-york-state/lake-placid-saranac-lake" class="copy--body__link">Lake Placid</a>, NY, and explore the tracks that wend through 6 million acres (24,200 sq km) of fantastic forest in the Adirondack Mountains to experience an explosion of leaf-turning colour.</p><p><em><a href="http://www.lakeplacid.com/" target="_blank" class="copy--body__link">Lakeplacid.com</a>; Watch the action from behind the sea stacks and spires on Bullards Beach.</p><p><em><a href="https://www.lonelyplanet.com/usa/pacific-northwest/oregon" class="copy--body__link">Oregon</a>; free.</em></p></div>
